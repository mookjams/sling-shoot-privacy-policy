Privacy Policy
Effective Date: March 2026
1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how Slingshoot (“the App”) handles information.
Slingshoot is developed and published by Mark Jones (“we”, “our”, or “us”).
We respect your privacy. The App does not collect personal information directly.

2. Information We Do Not Collect
We do not:
Require account registration
Collect names, emails, or phone numbers
Collect precise location data
Sell or share personal information
Use third-party advertising networks

3. Apple Services
The App may use Apple-provided services such as:
Apple Game Center
Apple In-App Purchases
Apple Analytics
These services are operated by Apple and may collect data according to Apple’s own Privacy Policy.
We do not have access to personal data collected by Apple beyond what Apple provides to developers in aggregated or anonymized form.
You can learn more about Apple’s privacy practices at:
https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

4. Gameplay Data
Gameplay progress, scores, and preferences may be stored locally on your device.
If Game Center is enabled, leaderboard data may be associated with your Apple Game Center account.

5. Children’s Privacy
Slingshoot does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
If you believe personal information has been collected unintentionally, please contact us.

6. Data Security
Because we do not collect personal information directly, we do not store personal user databases.
All Apple services are secured by Apple’s infrastructure.

7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by revising the effective date above.
